Investor Information

Allting Infrastructure Fund

A private equity fund investing in AI infrastructure and operating companies across emerging markets.

Fund Overview

Hybrid Infrastructure Investment

The Fund provides investors with exposure to the AI infrastructure layer serving emerging markets through a combination of platform revenue share and direct equity investments in operating companies.

HoldCo

Owns and operates the shared infrastructure platform. Generates recurring revenue through a 15% revenue share from all operating companies.

Fund

Invests directly in operating companies building AI products on the Allting platform. Provides growth capital and strategic support to scale across emerging markets.

Investment Thesis

Why AI Infrastructure. Why Emerging Markets. Why Now.

01

Structural Tailwinds

6.8 billion people lack meaningful AI access. Mobile penetration and digital payment infrastructure in emerging markets have reached inflection points that make AI distribution viable at scale.

02

Capital-Efficient Model

The platform is built once and shared across all operating companies. Each incremental OpCo generates revenue at near-zero marginal cost, creating exceptional operating leverage.

03

First-Mover Advantage

No comparable shared AI infrastructure exists for emerging markets. Early positioning creates network effects and switching costs that compound over time.

04

Diversified Revenue Base

Revenue is generated across multiple operating companies, geographies, and verticals, reducing single-company concentration risk while maintaining platform-level economics.
