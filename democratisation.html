AI Democratisation

Bringing AI to the next billion users

The Access Gap

6.8 billion people have limited or no access to AI. Premium AI services are priced for high-income markets — $20/month when the average salary in target markets is $375-600/month. The gap between AI-rich and AI-poor nations is 10.6 percentage points and widening.

Infrastructure, Not Charity

Allting's approach to democratisation is economic, not philanthropic. By building shared infrastructure that reduces cost-per-user to viable emerging-market price points, we create the conditions for a self-sustaining AI ecosystem. When AI costs dropped 60x in 18 months, the economics crossed the viability threshold.

The Operator Model

Just as telecom infrastructure enabled hundreds of mobile operators to serve billions of users, Allting enables AI operating companies to serve markets that big AI won't. One platform. Many operators. Each adapted to local needs, languages, and use cases.

Our Commitment

Allting is named after the Norse democratic assembly — the Althing — where every free person had a voice. We believe AI should not be a privilege of geography or income. Our infrastructure makes that possible at scale.
