Everything you need to know about Allting

Allting is the shared infrastructure layer for AI in emerging markets. We provide everything an AI company needs to launch and scale — model access, API gateway, billing, security, compliance, and analytics — so they can focus on their product and customers.

An Operating Company (OpCo) is an independent AI business that builds and sells products on the Allting platform. OpCos keep 85% of their revenue and benefit from shared infrastructure, reducing their time-to-market from months to hours.

We take a 15% revenue share from every OpCo on the platform. As more OpCos join, our revenue grows with near-zero marginal infrastructure cost — creating operating leverage similar to tower companies in telecom.

We focus on emerging markets — Latin America, MENA (Middle East & North Africa), Southeast Asia, and Sub-Saharan Africa. These regions represent 1.7 billion potential users with growing demand for AI but no infrastructure layer to serve them.

Allting provides access to all major AI models including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta (LLaMA), Mistral, and DeepSeek. Our intelligent routing optimizes for cost and performance across models.

Hyperscalers sell raw compute and model access to developers. Allting provides a complete operator platform — including billing, compliance, localization, and revenue sharing — designed specifically for companies building AI products in emerging markets.

Allting is built by a team with 30+ years of telecom infrastructure experience (Ericsson, TeliaSonera, Millicom, du/EITC), combined with entrepreneurial and fund management expertise across EMEA.

Contact us at info@allting.ai to discuss becoming an OpCo on the platform.
