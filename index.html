About Allting

The TowerCo for AI

"American Tower doesn't make phones. They own the towers every carrier needs.

We don't make AI apps. We run the platform every AI company needs."

What We Do

Allting builds and operates the shared infrastructure layer that enables AI companies to launch and scale products in emerging markets -- without building the underlying platform from scratch.

How We Earn

We take a 15% revenue share from every operating company (OpCo) on our platform. We don't build AI products. We enable others to build and sell them.

Why It Works

Every new OpCo increases revenue with almost no increase in cost. The platform is built once. The economics compound with every partner added.
