The Company

About Allting

Allting was founded to de-layer AI infrastructure in emerging markets -- making it possible for independent operating companies to build and scale AI products without rebuilding the platform from scratch.

The name "Allting" comes from the Norse democratic assembly, the Althing -- one of the oldest parliaments in the world. It reflects our belief that AI should be accessible to all, not concentrated among a few.

Headquartered with operations across Latin America and the Middle East, Allting serves as the shared infrastructure layer connecting AI providers with the next billion users.
